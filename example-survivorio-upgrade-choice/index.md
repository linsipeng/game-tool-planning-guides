# Survivor.io Upgrade Choice Worked Example

## Scenario

A player can afford either three smaller weapon upgrades or one major equipment breakpoint. The purpose is to compare the next playable stage, not lifetime spending.

## Inputs

| Option | Cost | Expected gain |
| --- | ---: | --- |
| Three weapon levels | 18,000 | Smooth incremental damage |
| Equipment breakpoint | 20,000 | New multiplier tier |
| Available currency | 21,500 | Leaves different reserves |

## Decision

Choose the breakpoint only if the stage-specific output gain exceeds the weapon path and the remaining reserve covers the next mandatory upgrade.

## Verification

Enter both scenarios separately in [Survivor.io Calculator](https://survivoriocalc.com). Keep stage, character, and equipment assumptions identical so the difference represents only the upgrade choice.
