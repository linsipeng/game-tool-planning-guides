# Nuzlocke Planning Misconceptions

## “A route always equals one random encounter”

Rulesets may treat gifts, static encounters, floors, or named subareas differently. Record the actual clause before reserving a route.

## “The strongest available Pokémon should always be used”

A strong member can break the next level cap or remove experience needed by a weaker role. Availability and cap legality matter as much as raw strength.

## “A lost team member only changes one battle”

Losses can invalidate later pivots, encounter reservations, and experience allocation. Rebuild every dependent step.

## “Dupes clause always means a free reroll”

Some rules require a reroll, some allow it, and some limit attempts. The selected interpretation must be explicit.

## “A boss plan is complete once type matchups are covered”

Speed, setup, critical hits, status, and switch safety also need contingencies.
