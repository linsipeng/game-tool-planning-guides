## Automatic cut rules

- If arrival reaches the latest-safe time, remove the next optional stop.
- Keep at least five minutes unassigned for loading and transition delays.
- Do not place two fixed group activities back-to-back without a joining buffer.
- Record the last acceptable session end time before starting.

Confirm the relevant cycles with [Sky Clock Online](https://skyclockonline.com), but keep the plan usable when a transition or group join takes longer than expected.
