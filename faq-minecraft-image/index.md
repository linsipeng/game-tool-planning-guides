# Minecraft Image Conversion FAQ

## Why did transparent areas become solid blocks?

The source may lack a usable alpha channel or transparency was flattened during import.

## Should dithering always be enabled?

No. Compare it on and off, especially for icons, text, and small logos.

## How do I prevent stretching?

Preserve the source aspect ratio when selecting output dimensions.

## Why are impractical blocks in the material list?

The selected palette may include creative-only, expensive, directional, or unavailable blocks.

## What should be checked before building?

Dimensions, orientation, silhouette, transparency, palette, and material quantities.

## How can I preserve a small emblem?

Protect its region, reduce dithering, or increase output resolution.
