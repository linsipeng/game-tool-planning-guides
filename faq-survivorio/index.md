# Survivor.io Upgrade Comparison FAQ

## What cost should two options use?

Use complete path cost to the target state, including prerequisites.

## Why is next-level cost different from lifetime cost?

One is the immediate purchase; the other includes historical spending.

## Does a breakpoint always beat smaller upgrades?

No. It may scale better later while smaller upgrades solve the current stage sooner.

## Why record remaining currency?

The leftover balance determines whether the next required upgrade remains possible.

## How do I make two scenarios comparable?

Keep character, stage, equipment, buffs, and output metric identical.

## Should future and current builds be mixed?

No. Report immediate output separately from a saved future target.
