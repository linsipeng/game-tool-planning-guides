# HSR Warp Budget Comparison

| Strategy | Featured certainty | Reserve protection | Flexibility | Best use |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| One five-star ceiling | Low without guarantee | Medium | High | Accept either outcome |
| Full featured guarantee | High | Low | Low | Must-have character |
| Fixed pull stop | Variable | High | High | Protect future banner |
| Reserve-first budget | Variable | Highest | Medium | Multiple banner plan |
| Early-result stop | Result-dependent | High | High | Avoid impulse continuation |

## Decision rules

- Calculate from current pity and guarantee.
- Remove reserve before defining spendable passes.
- Model the lost featured outcome.
- Pause after every five-star.
