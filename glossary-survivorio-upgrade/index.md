# Survivor.io Upgrade Terms

- **Current level:** Starting level before the compared upgrade.
- **Next-level cost:** Cost of one immediate level.
- **Path cost:** Total cost of all steps needed to reach a target.
- **Lifetime cost:** Cumulative historical spending, not the next upgrade price.
- **Breakpoint:** Level or tier where a new effect or multiplier activates.
- **Prerequisite:** Required earlier upgrade included in the path.
- **Remaining currency:** Balance after completing an option.
- **Scenario:** Fixed character, stage, equipment, and other assumptions.
- **Output metric:** Damage, survivability, or another consistent comparison value.
- **Future build:** Planned later state that should be separated from current output.

Keep scenarios identical when comparing paths in [Survivor.io Calculator](https://survivoriocalc.com).
