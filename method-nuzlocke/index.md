# Nuzlocke Route Planning Methodology

## State model

Treat the run as a changing inventory of legal party members, remaining encounters, level caps, and ruleset clauses. A loss or capture changes more than one future decision.

## Evaluation order

1. Confirm rules and active cap.
2. Remove unavailable members and encounters.
3. Identify roles required by the next major battle.
4. Compare current-box coverage.
5. Spend the least replaceable encounter only when a role remains missing.
6. Recheck experience distribution.

## Assumptions

- Encounter legality follows the recorded ruleset.
- A planned role is not considered available if its only member exceeds the cap.
- Guaranteed encounters have higher option value than broad random routes.
- Critical-hit and setup contingencies are part of the plan, not optional notes.

Use [Nuzlocke Guide](https://nuzlockeguide.com) to keep the state synchronized after every material event.
