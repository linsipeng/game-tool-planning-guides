# Nuzlocke Planning Terms

- **Level cap:** Maximum permitted level before a specified boss.
- **Dupes clause:** Rule allowing or requiring a repeat encounter to be rerolled.
- **Encounter:** The catch opportunity assigned to a route or area.
- **Guaranteed encounter:** A species or gift that does not depend on a random roll.
- **Pivot:** A party member used to switch safely between matchups.
- **Cleaner:** A fast or priority attacker intended to finish weakened threats.
- **Death box:** Record of Pokémon no longer legal for use.
- **Route reservation:** Choosing not to consume an encounter until a later need is known.
- **Over-levelled:** Above the active cap and therefore unavailable under the ruleset.
- **Recovery plan:** Revised encounter and boss plan after a loss.

Record clause variations before interpreting a route recommendation. [Nuzlocke Guide](https://nuzlockeguide.com) can be used to rebuild the current run state with those definitions.
