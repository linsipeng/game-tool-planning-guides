# Nuzlocke Route Option Comparison

| Option | Reliability | Experience risk | Encounter cost | Best use |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Use current roster | High if roles exist | Medium | None | Preserve future routes |
| Take guaranteed encounter | High | Low | Consumes reserved asset | Missing critical role |
| Roll random route | Variable | Low | Consumes route | Several acceptable species |
| Train backup member | High | High near cap | None | Existing box coverage |
| Delay optional boss | Rules-dependent | Low | Time only | More information available later |

## Decision rules

- Prefer the current roster when it covers lead, pivot, setup response, and cleanup.
- Spend a guaranteed encounter when the missing role is specific and high impact.
- Avoid training that pushes another required member over the cap.
- Record how each choice changes later routes.
