# Minecraft Image Conversion Misconceptions

## “A transparent background is just another colour”

Transparency should produce no block; flattening it to a colour changes the build.

## “More dithering always improves the image”

Dithering can destroy small logos and increase visual noise at block scale.

## “Changing width alone cannot distort the source”

If aspect ratio is not preserved, the output stretches even when the requested width is correct.

## “The closest colour is always a practical block”

Creative-only, expensive, directional, or unavailable blocks may violate the intended palette.

## “The preview is enough to begin building”

Material counts, orientation, dimensions, and transparent regions also need verification.
