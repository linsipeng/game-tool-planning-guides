# HSR Warp Planning FAQ

## Is pity shared between character and light-cone banners?

No. Use history from the matching banner category.

## How do I account for a guaranteed featured character?

Record guarantee as a separate state and clear it only after the applicable five-star result.

## What is the difference between available and spendable passes?

Spendable passes exclude the reserve protected for future plans.

## Should I keep pulling after an early five-star?

Pause first. The result changes pity, guarantee, and the value of the remaining budget.

## Can probability replace a hard budget?

No. Probability describes likelihood; a stop rule enforces protection.

## What should I save after a session?

Remaining passes, ending pity, guarantee state, and the exact stop reason.
