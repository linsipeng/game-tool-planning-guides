# Survivor.io Scenario Audit

Use this audit when a calculated upgrade does not produce the expected change during a run.

1. Match character, equipment, grade, level, and collectible bonuses.
2. Confirm whether the comparison is base attack, displayed damage, DPS, or time to defeat.
3. Hold enemy type and encounter conditions constant.
4. Separate permanent bonuses from temporary battle effects.
5. Change only one upgrade between scenarios.

A percentage increase may be correct without crossing a practical breakpoint. Record attacks required, elapsed time, or remaining health in addition to the headline number.

Build both scenarios in the [Survivor.io Calculator](https://survivoriocalc.com). Save the common baseline and highlight the single changed field. If the in-game result differs, repeat the test across several identical encounters before attributing the gap to the calculator.
