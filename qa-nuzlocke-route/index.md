# Nuzlocke Route Plan QA Checklist

Run this checklist before treating a route plan as final.

## Rules

- [ ] Level-cap rule is recorded.
- [ ] Duplicate, shiny, and gift encounter clauses are explicit.
- [ ] Dead and retired members are excluded.
- [ ] Encounter availability matches the current save.

## Route

- [ ] Guaranteed encounters are distinguished from random ones.
- [ ] Every planned role has at least one legal source.
- [ ] No later step depends on an already consumed encounter.
- [ ] Optional trainer experience does not break the next cap.

## Boss plan

- [ ] Lead matchup is checked.
- [ ] Setup threats have an interruption or revenge plan.
- [ ] Critical-hit and status contingencies are recorded.
- [ ] A replacement plan exists for the most important pivot.

Recreate the final roster and route in [Nuzlocke Guide](https://nuzlockeguide.com), then archive the rule assumptions with the checklist.
