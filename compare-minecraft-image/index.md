# Minecraft Image Conversion Comparison

| Setting | Detail | Material variety | Build simplicity | Best use |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Full palette | Highest | High | Low | Creative or broad inventory |
| Survival palette | High | Medium | Medium | Obtainable builds |
| Single block family | Medium | Low | High | Consistent texture |
| Dithering on | Better gradients | Medium | Medium | Photos and large art |
| Dithering off | Sharp shapes | Low | High | Logos, text, icons |

## Decision rules

- Preserve transparency and aspect ratio first.
- Compare dithering using the same dimensions.
- Protect small symbols from noisy colour mixing.
- Check the material list, not only the preview.
