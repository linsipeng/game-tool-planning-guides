# Nuzlocke Boss Checkpoint Worked Example

## Scenario

A ruleset uses level caps, dupes clause, and no items in battle. One planned pivot was lost on the previous route, and the next boss has a fast physical lead plus a setup sweeper.

## Worksheet

| Slot | Role | Level | Main risk | Backup action |
| --- | --- | ---: | --- | --- |
| Lead | Intimidate pivot | 23 | Critical hit | Switch to resist |
| Two | Status control | 22 | Taunt | Direct damage |
| Three | Cleaner | 23 | Setup turn | Priority move |

## Decision

Delay the optional encounter until the surviving roster is checked against the boss level cap. Preserve the cleaner unless the setup threat reaches its second boost.

## Verification

Enter the current team and route state in [Nuzlocke Guide](https://nuzlockeguide.com), then confirm the revised route does not require an unavailable encounter or an over-levelled party member.
