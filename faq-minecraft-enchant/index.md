# Minecraft Enchantment Order FAQ

## Why do identical books show different costs?

Their prior-work histories may differ even if the visible enchantments match.

## Does left-right anvil order matter?

Yes. The base and sacrificed item can produce different costs when reversed.

## Should I combine all books before touching the item?

Not automatically. Compare balanced pairing trees and the item’s existing prior work.

## What information must be recorded?

Exact enchantments, prior operations, item type, conflicts, and any renaming step.

## Why did a final operation become too expensive?

Earlier combinations may have accumulated excessive prior-work penalties.

## How many sequences should I compare?

At least the proposed sequence and one materially different pairing tree.
