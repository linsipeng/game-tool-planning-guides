# Genshin Damage Calculation Misconceptions

## “More crit damage always means a better build”

Crit rate, attack, buffs, and rotation frequency determine whether extra crit damage improves expected output.

## “The largest critical hit is the best comparison”

Expected average and repeatable rotation damage are generally more useful than one critical result.

## “Energy recharge does not affect damage calculations”

It may not change one hit, but it changes how often a burst appears in a repeatable rotation.

## “All damage bonuses are interchangeable”

Bonuses apply only to eligible damage categories and may be additive with some fields rather than multiplicative.

## “Two calculator results are comparable if the character build differs”

Enemy level, resistance, reactions, buffs, and talent assumptions must also match.
