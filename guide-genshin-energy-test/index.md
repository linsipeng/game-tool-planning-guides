# Comparing Genshin Builds Without Ignoring Energy

A build that wins one damage screenshot may lose across a full rotation if it cannot recover its burst. Energy requirements should be part of the test case, not an afterthought.

Begin with a fixed rotation and count how often each character uses a skill, catches particles, and receives external energy. Decide whether the comparison represents one chamber, repeated chambers, or sustained combat. These contexts can favor different artifact and weapon choices.

For each build, record damage, rotation duration, and whether the next rotation starts on time. If a low-recharge build needs extra skills or waiting, include that time in the comparison. A slightly smaller burst that repeats reliably can produce more useful total output.

Enter identical enemy, buff, and rotation assumptions in the [Genshin Impact Damage Calculator](https://gidamagecalc.com). Change only the build variables under review. The conclusion should state both the damage difference and the energy condition required to achieve it.
