# Genshin Damage Input Field Reference

| Field | Meaning | Common mistake |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Character level | Attacker level used in defense math | Recording only ascension |
| Talent multiplier | Multiplier for the tested hit | Selecting the wrong attack stage |
| Damage bonus | Relevant elemental or attack-type bonus | Adding unrelated bonuses |
| Critical state | Critical, non-critical, or average | Comparing a crit screenshot to average damage |
| Enemy resistance | Resistance after shred | Applying reduction twice |
| Reaction state | Aura, trigger, and reaction type | Omitting elemental order |
| Active buffs | Buffs present at the exact hit | Including expired rotation buffs |

Enter the controlled values in the [Genshin Impact Damage Calculator](https://gidamagecalc.com). Change one field at a time and keep the baseline with the result.
