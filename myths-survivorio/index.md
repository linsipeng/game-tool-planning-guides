# Survivor.io Upgrade Calculation Misconceptions

## “The displayed upgrade price is the full path cost”

Several prerequisite levels may be required before the target breakpoint.

## “Lifetime cost and next-level cost can be compared directly”

They answer different questions and must use a common basis.

## “A higher tier always improves the current stage more”

Some breakpoints favour future scaling while smaller upgrades may solve the immediate stage.

## “Remaining currency does not affect the decision”

An option that empties the balance can block a mandatory follow-up upgrade.

## “Two screenshots are enough for a fair comparison”

Character, stage, equipment, and output metric must remain identical.
