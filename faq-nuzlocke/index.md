# Nuzlocke Route Planning FAQ

## When should I reserve an encounter?

Reserve it when a future boss needs a role that current captures cannot safely cover, especially if the encounter is guaranteed.

## What changes after a party member dies?

Remove every later step that depends on its typing, speed, ability, or switch role, then recalculate experience and encounter needs.

## How should level caps be recorded?

Store the active boss, exact cap, and whether reaching the cap is allowed or only exceeding it is banned.

## Can gift Pokémon and static encounters share an area?

That depends on the ruleset. Record gifts, statics, and random encounters as separate or shared before planning.

## What is a useful boss-plan minimum?

A lead, switch plan, setup response, critical-hit contingency, and cleanup route.

## How often should the route be recalculated?

After every capture, death, unexpected level gain, or rule clarification.

Maintain the current state in [Nuzlocke Guide](https://nuzlockeguide.com).
