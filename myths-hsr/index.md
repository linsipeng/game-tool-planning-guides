# HSR Warp Planning Misconceptions

## “Current pity is shared across every banner”

Banner categories maintain separate histories. Use pity from the matching banner type.

## “Having enough for one five-star guarantees the featured character”

Without an active guarantee, the first five-star can lose the featured outcome.

## “Reserve currency is available until it is actually spent”

A reserve only works if it is removed from the spendable budget before the session.

## “Early success means the remaining budget is safe to keep spending”

Early results change pity and guarantee state. Pause and recalculate before continuing.

## “Probability is a stopping rule”

A probability estimate does not protect a future banner. Use a fixed pull or reserve boundary.
