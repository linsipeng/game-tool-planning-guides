# Genshin Build Comparison Matrix

| Build focus | One-hit output | Expected average | Rotation consistency | Best use |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| High crit damage | Very high crit | Variable | Medium | Showcase hit with enough rate |
| Balanced crit | High | High | High | General repeatable damage |
| Energy recharge | Lower hit | Medium | Very high | Burst every rotation |
| Attack-heavy | High baseline | Medium | High | Low external attack buffs |
| Reaction-focused | Contextual | Contextual | Team-dependent | Reliable reaction setup |

## Decision rules

- Compare identical enemies, buffs, and talent levels.
- Evaluate two or more rotations when energy differs.
- Save non-critical, critical, and expected-average values.
- Separate personal damage from team reaction contribution.

Test controlled builds in [Genshin Damage Calculator](https://gidamagecalc.com).
