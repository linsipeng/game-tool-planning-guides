# Genshin Damage Input Terms

- **ATK:** Final attack value used before talent and damage multipliers.
- **Crit rate:** Probability that an eligible hit becomes critical.
- **Crit damage:** Extra critical multiplier, entered separately from crit rate.
- **Damage bonus:** Elemental, physical, normal, skill, burst, or other applicable bonus.
- **Resistance:** Target mitigation associated with the damage type.
- **Defence multiplier:** Level-dependent target defence adjustment.
- **Expected average:** Weighted result using non-critical and critical outcomes.
- **Reaction:** Additional or transformed damage caused by elemental interaction.
- **Snapshot:** A skill retaining selected buffs from its cast moment.
- **Rotation damage:** Sum of relevant attacks across a repeatable sequence.

Use consistent enemy and buff assumptions when entering these fields in [Genshin Damage Calculator](https://gidamagecalc.com).
