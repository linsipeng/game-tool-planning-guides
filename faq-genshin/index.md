# Genshin Damage Comparison FAQ

## Should I compare critical hits or average damage?

Use expected average for consistency, then consider full rotation frequency and energy requirements.

## Why did higher crit damage lower the average?

The build may have lost crit rate, attack, damage bonus, or a conditional passive.

## Does energy recharge belong in a damage comparison?

Yes when it changes whether a burst can be used every rotation.

## Why do two calculators disagree?

Check talent scaling, enemy defence, resistance, reactions, buff stacking, and percentage formats.

## Should reaction damage be combined with the triggering hit?

Record both components according to the reaction type and comparison question.

## What is the best baseline test?

Remove conditional buffs, fix one enemy, and save non-critical, critical, and expected-average values.
