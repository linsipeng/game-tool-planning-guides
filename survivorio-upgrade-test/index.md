## Practical adjustment

Ask:

1. Does the bonus apply during the part of the stage that causes failure?
2. Does it require kills, low health, stationary uptime, or a narrow trigger?
3. Does the comparison assume permanent uptime that the stage cannot provide?
4. Does more damage sacrifice survival or wave control?

## Decision record

Choose the upgrade that addresses the scenario, not the one with the largest unrelated showcase total. Save the assumptions so later upgrades can be compared against the same baseline.
