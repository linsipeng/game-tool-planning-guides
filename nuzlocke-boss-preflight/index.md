## Opponent map

For every opposing Pokémon, record:

1. Its most dangerous move.
2. The teammate that can switch into that move.
3. A second response if the first answer is weakened.
4. Speed uncertainty.
5. A critical hit, status, setup, or trapping condition that changes the plan.

## Team dependency check

Count how often each teammate appears as the only safe response. If one Pokémon answers most threats, the plan is fragile even when the matchup looks favorable.

Replace a redundant attacker with a defensive bridge when possible. Useful bridges provide an immunity, resistance, Intimidate, reliable recovery, status control, or a safe pivot into the main attacker.
